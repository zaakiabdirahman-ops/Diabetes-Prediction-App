üî¨ What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body processes blood sugar (glucose).

  • Type 1: Autoimmune condition where pancreas produces little to no insulin
  • Type 2: Body becomes resistant to insulin or doesn't produce enough
  • Prediabetes: Blood sugar levels higher than normal but not yet diabetic
